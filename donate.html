General Needs

We want every child and youth in our program to have the opportunity to grow, learn, and thrive. Your generosity helps provide children and youth with the essential care and support they need to build a brighter future.

General funds are used to meet urgent needs, supplement educational and medical expenses, support children and youth who have joined the program while they wait for sponsorship, and help cover the administrative costs of operating our non-profit.

Thank you for partnering with us to bring hope and lasting change.
